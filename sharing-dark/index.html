Writing about Dark

You can share your experience with Dark publicly. Here's a couple examples of users sharing about Dark:

  • Kevin Hoffman's blog about IoT sensors
  • Tair's snipy tool for Slack.
  • The best way to share code for now is often via screenshot (ex: Ellie's Project).
  • If you tag us on Twitter, @darklang we'll try to RT.

Feel free to let your readers know that they can ask you for an invite, or if you'd like a multi-use invite link, you can request one in the community Slack.

Giving talks about Dark

We've also seen users give talks about Dark at meetups and at work. If you'd like to do so, we've shared slide templates and logos to help make that easier. slide template.
